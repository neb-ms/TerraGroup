About TerraGroup

Terragroup, originally known as Terranova, was founded in the late 20th century as an environmentally focused corporation. It aimed to develop and implement innovative technologies to address global ecological challenges. Over time, Terragroup expanded its influence and diversified its activities, branching out into pharmaceutical research, weapons manufacturing, and more.
Terragroup played a significant role in the fictional city of Tarkov, which became a major industrial hub due to its strategic location. Tarkov was home to one of Terragroup's largest research facilities, known as the TerraGroup Labs. This corporation has conducted extensive experimentation and testing within these facilities, often involving advanced and experimental technologies.

Terragroup, in its pursuit of technological advancements, has developed various energy solutions:

  • Renewable Energy Technologies:

    Terragroup is dedicated to advancing renewable energy sources, such as solar power, wind power, and hydroelectric power. Our company has developed innovative solar panels, efficient wind turbines, and cutting-edge hydroelectric generators, harnessing clean and sustainable energy to power a wide range of applications.

  • Advanced Power Grid Systems:

    Terragroup specializes in designing and implementing advanced power grid systems that enhance energy distribution and efficiency. Our smart grid solutions optimize energy consumption, enable real-time monitoring of energy usage, and facilitate effective load management, thereby preventing blackouts and ensuring a stable power supply.

  • Energy Storage Solutions:

    Terragroup has invested in energy storage technologies to address the intermittent nature of renewable energy sources. These solutions include advanced battery systems and other energy storage devices that efficiently capture and store surplus energy during high production periods, enabling its release during peak demand periods.

  • Energy Management Software:

    Terragroup has developed sophisticated software systems for energy management. Their cutting-edge solutions offer real-time monitoring of energy usage, empowering businesses and individuals to optimize their energy consumption, identify inefficiencies, and reduce overall energy costs through data-driven insights.

  • Sustainable Infastructure Solutions

    Terragroup is actively engaged in creating sustainable infrastructure solutions. These include energy-efficient buildings, smart cities, and eco-friendly transportation systems. Their innovations prioritize energy conservation, utilization of renewable energy sources, and minimizing the environmental impact of infrastructure developments.

Energy Solutions

Terragroup's energy solutions aim to revolutionize the energy sector, driving the transition towards cleaner and more sustainable energy practices. Through their investments in research, development, and deployment of advanced technologies, Terragroup is committed to shaping a greener future.

Pharmaceutical Research

  • Medical Treatments

    Terragroup has focused on advancing medical treatments for various conditions. These treatments may include innovative therapies for combat-related injuries, advanced wound care solutions, or regenerative medicines aimed at accelerating healing and recovery processes.

  • Experimental Medications

    Terragroup's research has led to the development of experimental medications with significant effects on individuals. These drugs could encompass performance-enhancing substances, advanced painkillers, or stimulants designed to improve focus and combat effectiveness.

  • Combat Enhancements

    Terragroup has created drugs specifically designed to enhance the combat capabilities and survivability of military personnel. These drugs provide temporary boosts to strength, endurance, or cognitive abilities, improving a soldier's combat performance in high-intensity situations.

  • Biochemical Research

    Terragroup's long-term involvement in advanced biochemical research focuses on exploring new avenues of understanding human biology and developing drugs to manipulate biological processes. This research encompasses fields such as genetic engineering, personalized medicine, or novel drug delivery systems.

  • Chemical Agents

    Terragroup's pharmaceutical research extends to the development of chemical agents with diverse effects. These agents include incapacitating agents, truth serums, or hallucinogens, which have tactical applications or are subjects of experimentation.

LABS

The TerraGroup Labs facility, located within the city of Tarkov, is a sprawling complex dedicated to cutting-edge research and development. This state-of-the-art facility houses a network of advanced laboratories, each specializing in different scientific disciplines. The labs are equipped with state-of-the-art equipment and technology, including advanced testing apparatus, specialized containment units, and secure storage facilities. The facility's layout consists of interconnected corridors, high-security areas, and restricted access zones, reflecting the sensitivity and importance of the research conducted within. Within these labs, scientists and researchers delve into a wide range of fields, including pharmaceuticals, genetics, weapon systems, and advanced technologies. The labs serve as the epicenter of Terragroup's most secretive and controversial projects, where groundbreaking experiments and innovative discoveries push the boundaries of scientific knowledge and technological advancements.
